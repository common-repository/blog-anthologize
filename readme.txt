== Description ==


INCREASE FOUR TRAFFIC FROM SOCIAL BOOKMARKING SITS FOR MORE DETAILS VISIT: [Powerfull Social Bookmarking](http://www.blogform.co.cc/wordpress-plugins/powerfull-social-bookmarking-wordpress-plugin/ "Powerfull Social Bookmarking")

This plugin will add a image below your posts, allowing your visitors to share your posts with their friends, on THE BEST BOOKMARKING SITES TO GET HUGE TRAFFIC that is
1)FaceBook
2) Twitter
3) Myspace
4)Friendfeed
5)Technorati
6)del.icio.us
7) Digg, Google
8) Yahoo Buzz
9) StumbleUpon
 While the plugin is activated a  buttons will appear after the content of the post. Clicking this  button will bring the user to the  bookmarking site.  If the user isn't logged in they will be prompted to do so.  
Post your articles to the social bookmarking sites and get traffic.
